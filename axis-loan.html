Super Saver Home Loans

Only for major cities:-
  • Delhi NCR, Mumbai (incl Thane & Navi Mumbai), Bangalore, Pune, Kolkata, Chennai & Hyderabad.

Super Saver gives the customer an option to park additional funds which will reduce the interest obligation on the home loan to the extent of the funds parked, with the flexibility to withdraw the funds anytime. In order to provide the customers with the flexibility to redraw excess funds parked by him, Super Saver account comes with an ATM card, cheque book, internet and phone banking facility.

Features & Norms:
Loan Amount Rs 1 crore & above
Current Location Delhi & Mumbai (shall be extended to other locations subsequently
Tenure Upto 20 years (additionally upto 24 months moratorium for under construction properties)
Customer Type Resident Indians Only- Salaried & Self-Employed
Product Variants Vanilla Home Loan & Balance Transfer - Income Based Program
Principal reduction options a) EMI based: Works similar to a Standard Home Loan (Term loan). EMI is calculated (comprising of Principal & Interest) which is the maximum outflow. However, the actual outflow may be lesser than the EMI depending on the utilisation of principal (excess funds parked in the account) resulting in lower than the projected interest. b) Equated Principal Amortisation: The Principal repayment is distributed equally over the loan tenure & interest is payable on the actual utilisation of the principal.
Repayment No separate repayment mode is required to be collected. Customer needs to ensure that the funds equal to principal repayment & interest are available on the cycle date in Super Saver account.
FOIR 70% (10% lower FOIR under Equated Principal Amortisation)
Facilities to be issued Cheque Book, ATM card, Internet Banking & Phone Banking

Axis Bank Home Loan Eligibility Criteria

A) - Salaried Individuals
  • Individuals in permanent service in the Government or reputed companies

  • Professionals (ie, doctors, engineers, dentists, architects, chartered accountants, cost accountants, company secretary, and management consultants only) can apply.

Axis bank home loan procedure?

Once bank receive a completed application form along with the necessary supporting documents two things happen:

Determination of your eligibility for the Home Loan: For this bank consider internal policy guidelines and also examine all the documents submitted by you. If you are deemed eligible, you will get an approval for a specific loan amount based on your requirement, repayment capability and the value of the property.
Axis Bank will convey its decision within 30 working days from the date of receipt of the application provided the application is complete in all respects. The computation of 30 days shall starts from the day on which all documents required for a proper appraisal of the application are provided by the Customer to bank.
Legal and Technical verification: Lawyers and property valuers empanelled by the Bank will verify your title documents conduct a technical evaluation of the property.
Once both these are complete, the Bank will process the disbursement of your Home Loan.
